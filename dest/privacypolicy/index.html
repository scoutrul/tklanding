We automatically track and collect the following categories of information when you visit our Site, App or use our Services: (1) IP addresses; (2) domain servers; (3) types of devices accessing the Site and App; (4) types of web browsers used to access the Site (collectively “Traffic Data”); (5) data collected by Google Anayltics and similar platforms; (6) data from integrated Google+, Facebook APIs and Plugins for which you have provided permission. Traffic Data is anonymous information that does not personally identify you but is helpful for marketing purposes or for improving your experience on the Site and App, and when using our Services.

Cookies and web beacons are very small text files that are stored on your computer when you visit certain web pages. Teamkraft collects information through cookies and web beacons about your web browsing activities such as the address of the page you are visiting, the address of the referrer page you previously visited, the time you are viewing the page, your browsing environment and your display settings. We use cookies and web beacons on our Site, App and when you interact with our Services to:

  • Understand traffic patterns and the number of visitors to the Site, App and our Services and other non-Teamkraft websites that we partner with;

  • Understand how you use and interact with the Site, App and our Services;

  • Improve the Teamkraft products and services;

  • Optimize your experience on our Site, App and with our Services;

  • Provide anonymous individual or aggregate auditing, research, modeling and reporting for our advertisers and other partners (no personally identifiable information about you is shared with our advertisers and other partners as part of these activities);

  • Store your password so you do not have to re-enter it each time you visit the Site, App or use the Services.

  • Provide you relevant advertising and content; and

  • Properly manage our Site, App and our Services.

Please note that some cookies may be placed by a third party service provider who performs some of these functions for us. Other companies’ use of their cookies is subject to their own privacy policies, not this one.

If you are concerned about cookies, most browsers permit individuals to decline cookies. A user refusing cookies can still fully navigate our Site.
